Our Policy

At Maria.B Boutique, customer satisfaction is our priority. Please review our return and exchange policy carefully before making a purchase.

🔄 Exchange Policy

Items can be exchanged within 7 days of delivery. The product must be unused, unwashed, and in original packaging with tags attached.

❌ Return Policy

Returns are only accepted in case of defective, damaged, or incorrect items. Customers must report the issue within 48 hours of receiving the order.

💸 Refund Policy

Refunds are processed after inspection of returned items. It may take 5–10 business days for the amount to reflect in your account.

🚚 Shipping Charges

Shipping charges are non-refundable. Customers are responsible for return shipping costs unless the item is faulty.
