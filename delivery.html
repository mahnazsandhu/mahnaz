Shipping & Delivery Information

Shipping Policy

We ensure safe and timely delivery of your orders. Orders are processed within 1-3 business days.

Local delivery takes 3-5 working days. International orders may take 7-12 working days.

Shipping charges depend on your location. Free delivery is available on orders above PKR 5000.

We ship worldwide. Customs duties and taxes are the responsibility of the customer.

Once your order is shipped, you will receive a tracking number via email or SMS.

Delays may occur due to weather, holidays, or courier issues. We appreciate your patience.
